Goal 4

Develop strategies for classroom management and teachable moments.

Prepare for teachable moments. Practice for responses when students make derogatory statements or jokes that are harmful to groups.

If you have students that have physical limitations, have open conversations about it, don't “tip-toe” around the subject.

Create class jobs that help influence or assist a student with physical limitations. For example, students can take turns riding in the elevator with them or be the designated notetaker during class time.

When the students and teacher have an understanding and acceptance of this import, students are building empathy and competency. In return, these practices influence kindness, compassion and care in their day.
