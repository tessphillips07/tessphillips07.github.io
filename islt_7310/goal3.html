Goal 3

Teach and build cultural competency and empathy in young minds.

Have regular conversations throughout the school year about how to celebrate and value differences between students.

Take time to teach students about empathy and what it means. When we expose students to different worldviews, engage them with community projects and bring service learning into the classroom, they develop greater empathy and compassion.

When we practice self-awareness with students, this impacts their capacity to see themselves as uniquely different from other people. Engage them in reflective conversations about values, beliefs, attitudes, and moral dilemmas.

By encouraging them to do this, we let our students know that we value them.
