Goal 1

Curate a physical and emotional space where students feel represented but also learn further.

To create a welcoming physical environment, teachers must be prepared to reach outside of the normal classroom setup. Make sure students have access to books that not only their lives but also identities and perspectives outside their experiences.

Bulletin boards and signs get more attention than most think. Set up these displays to showcase different races, gender expressions and abilities.

Use slogans that encourage respect and empathy for all people. Emotionally, students can be fearful of being rejected based on their identities.

Set agreements on their first day to make it clear to students that put-downs or biased thinking is not welcome in the space.
